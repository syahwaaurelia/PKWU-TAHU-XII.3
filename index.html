How To Make Tahu Tek? this is the ingredients

300 grams of white tofu 100 grams of bean sprouts (boil for a while, drain) 2 small potatoes (steamed cooked, peeled) 1 small size cucumber 2 eggs (made into an omelet) 2 pieces of lontong/ketupat Enough fried shallots Enough chopped celery Sweet soy sauce

step by step

1. Crush fried peanuts, garlic, cayenne pepper and salt. Mix the paste into the ground nuts until evenly mixed. Mix in soy sauce to taste. 2. Cut the rice cake and tofu into squares. Cut the eggs according to taste. Usually, lontong, tofu and eggs are cut using scissors (hence the sound tek tek tek and then called tek tofu 3. Add the sprouts and cucumber on top of the lontong pieces, tofu and eggs 4. Drizzle with peanut sauce. Sprinkle fried onions. Ready to be served.
